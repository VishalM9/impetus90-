About The Fest

About The Fest

IMPETUS is our National level technical Extravaganza started in the year 2001. For eighteen years now, it has been serving as a platform for the students to showcase their skills and share their views. It brings together the brilliant minds, whose direction of thought has been streamlined by extensive knowledge, to compete in an arena marked by fight for creativity, excellence and innovation. Held on an inter-collegiate level, this fest sees a footfall of over 1000 students every year. IMPETUS is a technical fest designed for a change and with vivid difference.

Theme

RETRO REWIND
The past that never was
From calculator watches and classic gaming consoles to Polaroids and turntables, gadgets are guaranteed to deliver throwback fun. Older gadgets allow people to unplug themselves from being constantly dependent on smart devices which are in use today. As we all like to reminisce about how things were, it's time to bring back that feeling of nostalgia with retro technology.
We aim towards bringing out nostalgic and old school technology, which would enable the masses to disconnect from the present network without losing access of things they want.
